Welcome to Ademre!

Here you can be part of fan-art project, that aim to create version of universal, international sign language, that will help us present emotions, while we are covering our faces.

The main idea, as it will be on the picture underneath, once we'll get it done, is that there are three zones of gestures on your body.

The bad/angry one: begins on the level of your knees and goes up to belt. The lower you show the sign, the more contemptful it is.

The neutral zone: begins on the level of your hips and goes just above the belly button. This is a neutral zone, as your hand will be positioned there naturally. It does not have any "hidden" meaning

The full of respect zone: begins above your belly button and goes up to your chin. The higher you go, the more respectful signs become. Although some signs might need to be adjusted here, due to natural restrictions in humans hand movement.
